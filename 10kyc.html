Characters

















A monstrous guardian spends his days patrolling an isolated village,
protecting those within it. One day, he finds and scares off an outsider.
Little does he know this will be the last day he’ll see his people.
Some want him back, either so he can keep them safe,
or because they genuinely care for him.
Others are too mortified by the idea of his return.
The one thing they can agree on is that if they want him back,
they’ll need to find and search for him themselves…
But is it worth it?
